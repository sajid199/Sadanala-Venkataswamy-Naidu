Sadanala Venkataswamy Naidu

Multifaceted Artist & Social Contributor

Personal Profile

  • Birth Date: 15 February 1961
  • Birthplace: Gedellanka Village, East Godavari District
  • Nationality: Indian
  • Spouse: Dr. Madhuri
  • Parents: Satyavati and Balakrishna Rao
  • Other Names: Madhuri Swami, Sadhana

Academic Achievements

  • M.A. (Telugu) from Andhra University
  • Law Degree (B.L.) from G.S.K. Memorial Law College
  • B.Ed. from Annamalai University
  • M.Phil. in "Krishna Patrika Sahitiseva"
  • Ph.D. in Literature

Professional Journey

  • Chief Superintendent, South Central Railway
  • Lecturer at Kandukuri Veeresalingam Astika Degree College
  • First-class Telugu Scholar at Dornakal Railway High School

Literary Contributions

  • "Scene" (Poetry Collection)
  • "Krishnapatrika Sahitya Seva" (Theory Book)
  • "Naidu Bava Songs"
  • "Prayer of Unanimity"

Social Contributions

Committed to social service, Naidu provides scholarships to 20 underprivileged children annually through his "Big Help for Education" initiative. He actively supports educational and cultural programs in rural areas.

Awards and Honors

  • Golden Nandi Award (2012)
  • Best Teacher Award, Andhra Pradesh
  • Rotary Literary Award
  • Deccan Youth Poetry Festival Award
